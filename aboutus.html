Our History

Zenith started out as a group of friends refurbishing old shoes and reselling them as a way to pay off their school fees. They did this using money out of their own pockets, working from their homes. Over time, they started gaining popularity as people were spreading the word about their cheap, yet high quality products. Soon enough, this group of friends started their own little company using the money that they earned. They decided to branch out as a reseller for popular brands, using their already extensive network of followers.


Today, Zenith has thousands of fans all over the world, all eager to get their hands on the latest and greatest kicks. Though we started small, we have since grown into a large company providing shipping to almost all countries, all while preserving our values that started this all.

Our Values

Zenith is professional and committed in offering only the best kicks to our customers. The products sold on our store are guaranteed authentic kicks which come directly from original sources. We believe our honesty and high standards of before and after sales service will ensure you have the best stress-free shopping experience possible. We offer a wide selection of high-quality kicks, with new products arriving every day. Since 2017, Zenith is dedicated to satisfy the hype-hungry market with authentic kicks, just for you.
